Versatile Applications

Discover the diverse ways our state-of-the-art vertical wind tunnel is utilized by different flyers.

Recreation & Fun

Perfect for birthday parties, family gatherings, unique date nights, or checking off a bucket list experience in a safe environment.

Professional Sport

Indoor skydiving is a recognized sport. Professional flyers use wind tunnels to master advanced 3D maneuvers and formation flying.

Tactical Training

Military paratroopers and search & rescue teams utilize the wind tunnel to practice body stabilization and jump maneuvers with full combat gear.

Fitness & Therapy

Flying engages core muscles, improves balance, and serves as an inspiring adaptive recreation tool for individuals with physical challenges.

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before stepping into our flight chamber.

Yes, indoor skydiving is extremely safe. You will be accompanied by a certified flight instructor at all times inside the wind tunnel, and the environment is highly controlled. Full safety gear, including a helmet, goggles, and flight suit, is provided.
Flyers must be at least 3 years old. The maximum weight limit is typically 260 lbs (117 kg) for safety reasons, but please contact us if you have any questions or require special accommodations.
We recommend comfortable, casual clothes (like t-shirts and jeans or activewear) and lace-up sneakers. Avoid shoes with open toes, heels, or boots. We will provide the flight suit to wear over your clothing.
Each individual flight session lasts 60 seconds—which is actually longer than the freefall portion of a typical tandem skydive (about 45 seconds). Depending on your package, you will get multiple flight sessions.
While training and individual flight sessions inside the tunnel are done 1-on-1 with an instructor for safety, your group will be together in the flight chamber viewing area, and you will take turns flying.
