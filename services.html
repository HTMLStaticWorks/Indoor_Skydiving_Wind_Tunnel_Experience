Your Flight Journey

Here is what you can expect during your visit at AeroFly, from arrival to landing.

1
Check-In

Arrive 45 mins early, sign waivers, and meet your flight crew.

2
Flight School

Learn the basic body positions and hand signals from your instructor.

3
Gear Up

Get fitted with a flight suit, helmet, and safety goggles.

4
The Flight

Step into the wind tunnel and float on a cushion of air with your instructor.

5
Debriefing

Receive your flight certificate and view your high-definition flight media.
