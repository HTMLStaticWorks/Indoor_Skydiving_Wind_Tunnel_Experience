The Technology

Engineering the Impossible

160

MPH Top Speed

AeroFly was founded on a simple principle: everyone should experience the joy of flight. Our vertical wind tunnel (VWT) represents the pinnacle of aerodynamic engineering.

4x Axial Fans

Powered by massive electric motors generating over 1,500 horsepower.

Closed-Loop

Recirculating air system ensures perfect temperature and smooth airflow.

Unlike skydiving from an airplane, our tunnel provides a strictly controlled environment. Air is pushed up through the flight chamber grid, creating a wall-to-wall cushion of air that supports your body weight perfectly.

Academy progression

Training roadmap & levels

Discover how AeroFly Academy takes you from zero experience to a professional freefly master.

Level 1

Belly Flight Basics

Learn stable body positioning, basic hand signals, and how to execute controlled turns and height variations.

01
Time Required: 1-2 Hours
  • Neutral belly position
  • Forward & backward motion
  • 360-degree turns
Level 2

Back Flying & Transitions

Discover the freedom of flying on your back. Build the muscle memory to safely flip between belly and back flying.

02
Time Required: 5-8 Hours
  • Stable back flying posture
  • Belly-to-back transition loops
  • Air-docking with partner
Level 3

Vertical Dynamics (Head-Up)

Learn vertical flying. Suspended upright in a sitting position, requiring precise leg and arm air deflection.

03
Time Required: 15-20 Hours
  • Stable sit-fly positioning
  • Upright speed carvings
  • Vertical altitude control
Level 4

Pro Freeflying (Head-Down)

The ultimate level of flight control. Fly head-down, executing high-speed loops, carvings, and team choreography.

04
Time Required: 40+ Hours
  • Head-down speed carving
  • Multi-flyer coordination
  • Competitor-level acrobatics
